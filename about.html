About Us

UCUBE The Power of Possibilities

We at UCUBE are a one-stop destination for businesses looking for the best of IT services and IT solutions. We are a global IT Services and Solutions company, providing our clients with a wide range of services like Digital Transformation, Enterprise App Services, Outsourced Product Development, and Testing Centre of Excellence. Our goal is to solve various business problems faced by industries and providing them optimum and effective technology solutions. Our core competency includes Mobility Solutions, Business Analytics, Visualization and Collaboration, and Wireless & IP Communications. With adapting to the state-of-art-technologies, we plan to grow as a trusted brand for digital transformation solutions.

- We strive to be the industry standard in service to customers.

- We maintain a superior level of integrity in interactions with business partners and associates.

- We appreciate our achieved success and we conduct our business as model corporate citizens.

Our Vision

Ucube Technolgoies will design, develop, manufacture in house, inventory and distribute Optical Targeting and Accessories that support Close Tolerance Industrial Survey Systems such as Computer Aided Theodolites, Photogrammetry Systems, Laser Trackers and other emerging measurement systems. In addition we will be supporting those systems that have yet to be discovered. We understand and appreciate the many challenges the system users face regarding accuracy, complexity and deadline. We will support those users and their deadlines to the best of our ability.
